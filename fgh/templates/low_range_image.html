Extension: Image for low end of slider

URL for this extension: 

http://54.164.154.218:8000/fhir/StructureDefinition/questionnaire-lowRangeImage

Status: draft.

When a coded ordered value is selected from a range, indicates the image to the left of the first value; e.g. The "<img>" in "<img> 1 2 3 4 5 <img>".

Context of Use: Use on element: @Questionnaire.group.question

usage info: points to a location (URL) of the image
